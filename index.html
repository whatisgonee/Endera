Endera

Endera is a universe. Not ours, of course, but it is a universe in my mind. A universe which I build. A universe where even the weirdest stories can happen. It is my universe, and I welcome you to read about it. I write chapters, just like in a book, which tell you about different topics. Do you know what a wonder forest is? Or how we almost blew up the Sun? Or how we saved our home planet at the last possible moment? If you're interested, then go ahead.
