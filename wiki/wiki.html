Quick Review

When the science gets out of control. Endera is a scientific fiction, set in the far future, when humanity is slowly starting to colonize other solar systems. A new source of energy discovered, which is extremly chaotic and dangerous. When a corporation named 'Alter Worlds inc.' discoveres that the null-particles, the source of a huge amount of energy, come from an alternate world, thousands of ambitious people get attracted by the idea of interdimensioal travel. A scientific experiment breaks out of control, making an anomaly of null-particles, thus allowing the creatures from an alternate world enter our reality, which slowly starts to spread to solar system and, eventually, to Earth. In response to the alien creatures, countries from different planets organize a union. After establishing a group, called 'The Null Fighters', the leaders start the evacuation of the nearby solar systems.
